FOUNDER OF THE COMPANY

Mr. Kwame Opoku-Bamfoh

Ghanaian by birth, Mr. Kwame Opoku Bamfoh is the founder of the Premium Prendre Projects Finance Consortium Limited. The Founder having served as Foreman with NATAKOA CONSTRUCTION LTD [2000 - 2005] and HENSABICK CONSTRUCTION LTD [1996 - 2000] and has operated independently as Premium Prendre Projects Finance Consortium and Operations Ltd, Premium Prendre Construction and Premium Prendre Products a manufacturing and farming division.
